Platform Capabilities

Every feature built for Korean science workflows

From HWP parsing to PIPA compliance — UniPath handles what generic tools can't.

Parse HWP syllabi instantly

UniPath's Korean NLP engine reads HWP and PDF syllabi and extracts every deadline, grading weight, and lab session in under four seconds. Students spend zero minutes manually transcribing course requirements.

Eliminate missed deadlines automatically

UniPath pulls live assignment data from both u-SAINT and Blackboard and surfaces a unified deadline feed sorted by urgency, not by portal. Pilot data targets a reduction in missed assignment rates from 35% to under 10%.

Accelerate research lab placement 2.4×

The platform cross-references KAIST, POSTECH, UNIST, and DGIST lab directories against your transcript and research interests, then ranks openings by fit score. Students identified viable labs 2.4× faster than through manual outreach.

Complete exchange applications 65% faster

UniPath pre-fills exchange program forms using verified academic records from your SSO-authenticated profile and flags missing documents before deadlines close. Early testers completed full application packages in 3.1 hours instead of 8.7.

Authenticate once via university SSO

UNIST, KAIST, POSTECH, and DGIST SSO credentials log students into every connected system through a single sign-on — no duplicate accounts, no password resets. IT offices retain full session control and audit-log access.

Meet PIPA standards precisely

All student data is stored on Korean-domestic servers, encrypted at rest with AES-256, and processed under PIPA-compliant data handling agreements signed with each partner institution. University IT administrators receive a dedicated compliance dashboard.

How UniPath Works

From sign-in to full academic clarity in minutes

Four steps. No manual setup. No more juggling portals.

1

Connect your university account

Sign in once with your UNIST, KAIST, POSTECH, or DGIST SSO credentials. UniPath instantly authenticates and pulls your enrolled courses, pending assignments, and official academic calendar from u-SAINT and Blackboard.

2

Let AI read your syllabi

Upload or link your HWP and PDF syllabi and UniPath's Korean NLP engine extracts every deadline, assessment weight, and prerequisite within seconds. Your unified academic timeline is built automatically — no manual entry required.

3

Track deadlines across every system

A single bilingual dashboard displays all assignments, lab sessions, and administrative deadlines ranked by urgency and cross-referenced between u-SAINT and Blackboard in real time. Alerts fire precisely 72 hours and 24 hours before each deadline.

4

Discover labs and exchange programs

UniPath surfaces research lab openings and exchange program windows matched to your major, GPA tier, and stated research interests — ranked by fit score and sorted by application deadline.
